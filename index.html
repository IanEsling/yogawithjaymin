Welcome.

Jaymin Gansell teaches Hatha Yoga, her teaching being grounded in the Shadow Yoga system.

Jaymin teaches Shadow Yoga classes and workshops based in Northland. Classes are taught from a 7m yurt studio with views of the stunning Tutukaka coast.

Shadow Yoga workshops are also offered throughout Aotearoa where expressions of interest are shown, please get in touch.
