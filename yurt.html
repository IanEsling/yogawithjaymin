Yoga classes are held in a beautiful 7m yurt studio (with wooden floor) that has views of the stunning Tutukaka coast.

The yurt is situated halfway between Tutukaka and Matapouri along the Matapouri Rd, parking is next to the Mi Casa gallery at 539 Matapouri Rd, Tutukaka coast. Just follow the path up to the yurt.
